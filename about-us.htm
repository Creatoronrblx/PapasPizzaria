In the hot Chicago summer of 1980, Antonio "Papa" Russo stepped into his dream of owning his own pizzeria. With his mama's recipes and his father's business know-how, word of Papa's Pizzeria spread like marinara on spaghetti.

Today Papa Russo and his sons continue to delight their customers with the savory cuisine of Italy and Sicily. Come experience it for yourself at our location in the heart of our beloved Chicago!
